Exploration To Heaven🌡☄🌠

My spirit set forth to a journey of thy Uncalled😨
Emerging from my body and take the path of a road😵
A road narrow than a strand of hair
Slippery more than a bathing-hub🧼

Oh! What a call of devour While thy soul depart its beauty self👻
To a world believed to be purified without purification
Beautify with bonquet of flower🌹

Wine served in an hornet gold🍷
Food in merrisome with taste ever felt🍗🥧😋
Nor its aroma ionizes in mild air

A place we all pray😇
